I joined [SerpApi](https://serpapi.com/) last week as Python Developer Advocate.

SerpApi helps developers access search engine data programmatically across Google, Bing, DuckDuckGo, Amazon, and more via simple, reliable APIs.

Our APIs are used for:
- AI agents
- GEO and AI Overview use cases
- SEO
- background verification
- product research
- and more.

The part I am loving the most: SerpApi has a people first culture and the transparency here surprised me (in a positive way).
- Communications are mostly async. We are globally remote and distributed.
- High agency people are valued here - there is freedom to choose what to work on and when to work.
- Open books: Company data such as financials are shared with all the team members. Also, analytics, customer interactions, hiring processes, strategies, marketing, and all other business-related data are accessible to all team members.

I am new to Developer Advocacy, but I have loved parts of it such as giving talks, writing tech articles in the past and was always interested in exploring growth/community aspects. 

Writing this post during my onboarding - I am looking forward to getting started and doing great things here.
